Who We Are

Remova Inc. is an independent SAP Basis consulting and managed services provider. We deliver reliable SAP operations through disciplined processes, transparent reporting, and proven methodologies. Our focus is SAP infrastructure—the technical foundation that keeps your SAP systems running fast, secure, and available.

We work with organizations of all sizes, from single-system environments to complex global landscapes. Our clients value predictability, transparency, and verifiable results over generic success claims.

Operating Principles

Transparent Reporting

We provide monthly management reports with clear KPIs:

  • Incident counts by priority and category
  • SLA compliance percentage
  • System availability metrics
  • Change success rates
  • Root cause analysis for critical incidents

You know exactly what we deliver and where we stand against commitments.

Disciplined Change Management

Every production change follows structured process:

  • Business justification and risk assessment
  • Non-production testing first
  • Documented rollback procedure
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
  • Post-change validation
  • Complete audit trail

We minimize risk through process discipline, not luck.

Security-by-Design

Security controls are built into our operations:

  • Least privilege access principles
  • All actions logged and auditable
  • Encrypted remote access only
  • Regular security patch management
  • Backup verification and DR testing
  • Compliance documentation maintained

Security is not an add-on—it's how we operate.

Service Approach

What We Deliver

  • Structured Processes: Documented procedures for all common activities (patching, upgrades, changes, incidents)
  • Verifiable Results: Tangible deliverables like health check reports, RCA documents, configuration guides, runbooks
  • SLA Commitments: Clear response and resolution targets with monthly compliance reporting
  • Risk Control: Rollback plans, test scenarios, and validation procedures for all production changes
  • Knowledge Transfer: Documentation and training so your team understands what we do and why

What We Don't Do

  • We don't make unrealistic promises about "zero downtime" or "100% availability" without the infrastructure and cost to deliver it
  • We don't claim success based on unverifiable metrics or vague improvements
  • We don't implement changes without proper testing, documentation, and rollback plans
  • We don't hide problems or missed SLAs—we report transparently and work to improve

Our Philosophy: Reliable SAP operations come from disciplined process execution, not from claims of excellence. We let our deliverables, SLA compliance, and client systems speak for themselves.

Policies & Compliance

Data Protection & Privacy

We handle client data in accordance with applicable regulations:

  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (EU)
  • KVKK: Turkish Data Protection Law
  • Confidentiality: All client data protected under NDA
  • Data Minimization: We access only data required for specific tasks

Quality & Security Management

Our practices align with recognized frameworks:

  • ISO 27001 Principles: Information security management practices
  • SOX Controls: Change management and audit trail requirements for financial systems
  • ITIL Practices: Incident, problem, and change management processes

Business Continuity

We maintain operational resilience:

  • Distributed Team: Team members in multiple locations to mitigate site-specific risks
  • Documentation: All procedures documented and accessible to multiple team members

  • Escalation: Clear escalation paths for complex issues or staff unavailability
