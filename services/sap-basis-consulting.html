  • Database and OS platform selection guidance
  • Cloud migration feasibility and readiness assessment
  • Technical project management and coordination

Why It Matters

Proper SAP Basis architecture and operations planning directly impact:

  • System Uptime: Well-designed infrastructure reduces unplanned downtime and accelerates recovery
  • Performance: Correct sizing and configuration prevent bottlenecks before they occur
  • Total Cost: Right-sized infrastructure avoids over-provisioning while maintaining service levels
  • Security Posture: Proper architecture includes security controls at the infrastructure layer
  • Change Success Rate: Structured transport management reduces failed deployments
  • Audit Readiness: Documented architecture and processes satisfy compliance requirements

How We Do It

Step 1: Discovery & Documentation

  • Inventory all SAP systems, databases, and infrastructure components
  • Document current architecture diagrams and data flows
  • Review existing SLAs, RPO/RTO targets, and maintenance windows
  • Collect performance metrics and capacity utilization data

Step 2: Gap Analysis

  • Compare current state against SAP best practices and industry standards
  • Identify single points of failure and availability risks
  • Assess backup/recovery procedures against RPO/RTO targets
  • Evaluate change management and transport procedures

Step 3: Recommendations & Roadmap

  • Prioritize improvements by business impact and technical risk
  • Provide specific configuration recommendations with rationale
  • Estimate effort, timeline, and resource requirements
  • Define success criteria and validation procedures

Step 4: Implementation Support

  • Prepare detailed implementation plans with rollback procedures
  • Coordinate with application teams and infrastructure providers
  • Validate changes in non-production environments first
  • Document as-built configurations and updated procedures
